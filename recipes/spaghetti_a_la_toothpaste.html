Spagetti a la Toothpaste

Ingredients

A refreshing twist on Italian cuisine!

  • 1 box of spaghetti
  • 1 tube of mint-flavored toothpaste (or bubblegum flavor for a fun twist!)
  • Â½ cup mouthwash (optional, for extra freshness)
  • 1 tbsp crushed breath mints (for garnish)

Instructions:

  1. Cook the spaghetti according to the package instructions.
  2. Instead of tomato sauce, squeeze an entire tube of toothpaste over the noodles.
  3. Add mouthwash if you want your dish to have that zing factor.
  4. Toss the noodles to coat them evenly in minty madness.
  5. Sprinkle crushed breath mints on top for an elegant finish.
  6. Serve cold because heating it up might release toxic fumes.

Warning:

May freshen your breath permanently.
