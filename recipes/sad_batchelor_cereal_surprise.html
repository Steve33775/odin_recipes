Sad Batchelor Cereal Surprise

For those days when you just give up on cooking.

Ingredients

  • For those days when you just give up on cooking.
  • 2 cups of flat soda
  • 1 leftover slice of pizza
  • ½ cup of peanut butter (because why not?)
  • Optional: a handful of crushed pretzels for texture

Instructions:

  1. Pour the flat soda into a bowl (this is your "milk").
  2. Crush the stale chips into the bowl.
  3. Tear up the pizza slice and toss it in like cereal.
  4. Add a scoop of peanut butter on top like it’s gourmet.
  5. Stir aggressively while questioning your life choices.
  6. Eat directly from the bowl while sitting on the floor.

Pairs with:

Existential dread and regret.
